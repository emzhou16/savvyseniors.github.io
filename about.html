About Us

We are SavvySeniors

Our team, founded by three driven high school seniors: Emily Zhou, Eliza Kubicek, and Mirella Janssen, shares a passion for technology and community service.
Even as a team, while we each take on specific responsibilities within the organziation,
we prioritize a collaborative approach, addressing tasks and challenges with diverse perspectives.
Our emphasis is on collective brainstorming, constructive feedback, and
continuous improvement rather than on individual roles, which makes our organziation all the more unique and impactful.
