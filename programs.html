Our Programs & Events

Our Current Programs

SavvySeniors offers a variety of services tailored to our senior residents!

In Person Assistance

We proudly volunteer at three primary senior living communities: Arbor Glen, Stonecrest, and Norris Square. Our visits feature engaging presentations filled with games and distribute educational resources like brochures. We cover a wide range of technology topics, from beginner to advanced levels, ensuring personalized support across various devices.

Online Support

In addition to in-person support, SavvySeniors offers a variety of online resources to assist seniors. Our website provides accessible tech guides and tips, with 24/7 email support. We also offer a YouTube channel with free cell phone tutorials in Dutch, Chinese, and Spanish.

Community Impact

While we prioritize individualized support, we're also dedicated to expanding the SavvySeniors mission to a wider audience. Our goal is to make technology more accessible for seniors and provide the guidance they need to navigate online platforms with ease.
