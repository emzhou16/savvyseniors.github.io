Monthly Tech Tips

Hi there! I wanted to share a few tech tips that can make your digital life a bit easier and more enjoyable.


First, staying in touch with your loved ones has never been easier with video calls. Apps like Zoom, Skype, or FaceTime allow you to see and chat with family and friends, no matter the distance. It might seem a bit daunting at first, but once you get the hang of it, it's a great way to feel connected, especially if you can't meet in person often.


Next, if you haven't tried using a voice assistant like Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri, give it a shot! These handy tools can help you with daily tasks just by asking. Whether it's setting reminders, checking the weather, or even controlling the lights in your home, you can do it all without lifting a finger. It's a small change that can make a big difference in your day-to-day life.


Lastly, let's talk about staying safe online. It is important to use strong passwords and, if possible, set up two-factor authentication to keep your accounts secure.
